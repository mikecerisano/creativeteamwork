Insomnia

Insomnia is a dark comedy web series that explores complex themes through the life of Nikhil, a bisexual Indian-American writer and escort.

I served as the Director of Photography for this critically acclaimed series, creating the visual language that brought these complex characters to life. I also contributed significantly to the writing and production, though remained uncredited in these roles as it had a 2-year pre-production period and many roles were played by many people.

Critical Reception:

"You learn more about these characters in the short episodes than you do in many of the TV shows trending on Netflix." – Sharan Dhaliwal, Burnt Roti

"Addresses the concept of bi-erasure and its presence in our society and communities." – Brown Girl Magazine

"Balances [heavy scenes] expertly with his character Nikhil continuing to narrate through the stress." – The Nerdy Girl Express
